Investor Loans

What is a DSCR Loan?

A DSCR loan lets real estate investors qualify based on the rental income of the property — not their personal income. No W-2s. No tax returns. Just cash flow.

No Personal Income Required

Qualify based solely on the rental income of the subject property. Perfect for self-employed investors, retirees, and high net-worth borrowers who prefer not to disclose personal income.

Multiple Property Types

Eligible for single-family homes, 2-4 unit properties, condos, townhomes, and short-term rentals (Airbnb/VRBO). Expand your portfolio without income limitations.

Scale Your Portfolio Faster

Traditional loans limit how many properties you can finance. DSCR loans evaluate each property independently, letting you scale without hitting conventional loan limits.

Fast Closings

Because we're not waiting on tax returns and pay stubs, DSCR loans move faster. Typical closing time is 14–21 days through MME Home's wholesale lender network.

How the DSCR Ratio Works

Monthly Rental Income
Monthly Debt Obligations (PITIA)
A DSCR of 1.0 means the property breaks even — income covers the payment.
Most lenders require 1.0 or higher. A ratio of 1.25+ gets the best rates.
